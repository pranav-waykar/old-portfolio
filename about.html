I'm a UX Practitioner based in Pune, India

Hello there! I'm Pranav Waykar, a Senior User Experience Designer based in Pune, India. I am currently working on designing two digital products under the ZAIDYN platform at ZS Associates.

I'm a designer who's not afraid to get my hands dirty with code. With a background in computer engineering and experience working as a software developer at Infosys Limited, I bring a technical perspective to my design work. Combining my technical expertise with my design skills, I craft solutions that are both practical and visually appealing.

While working at Infosys, I had the privilege of participating in the Strategic Design Program at the Center of Complexity at Rhode Island School of Design (RISD). The program focused on how to approach complex systemic problems in a structured manner, and to design interventions that have a measurable impact. This unique skillset has proven invaluable in my work as a UX Practitioner, allowing me to navigate challenging problem spaces where information is ambiguous or incomplete.

When I am not geeking out over UX, I like to take long walks on the nearby hills and indulge in my passion for reading.

If you'd like to chat, feel free to drop me a line at pranav.waykar@gmail.com
